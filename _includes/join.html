Above all of that, though, we as a group understand that you play games, Milsims being no exception, to have fun. We pride ourselves on calm professionalism, not forcing you to go through pointless trainings with in-game PT or drill, nor will we pressure you to do something you don't want to. Instead, you'll be held to a high standard as an individual, with cultural integration and attitude being strong principles for us.

You'll be more than welcome in the unit, provided that:

  • You're 18 or older
  • You have a working microphone
  • You're able to attend ops held in evenings, European time, once a week

Onboarding is as simple as being screened by way of a brief, painless form that won't take more than a couple of minutes, and from thereon you'll be able to attend operations at your discretion.
